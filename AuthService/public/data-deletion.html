How to Delete Your Data

Option 1: Direct Request

To request deletion of all your data from our service, click the button below:

Request Data Deletion

Option 2: Facebook Users

If you signed up using Facebook, you can also delete your data through Facebook by:

  1. Going to your Facebook Settings
  2. Selecting "Apps and Websites"
  3. Finding our app in the list
  4. Selecting "Remove" to delete your data

Option 3: Contact Us

You can also email us at [Your Contact Email] to request data deletion. Please include:

  • Your full name
  • Email address used with our service
  • Whether you used social login (Google/Facebook)

Note: Data deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Your account and all associated data will be removed from our systems.

For more information about how we handle your data, please see our Privacy Policy.
